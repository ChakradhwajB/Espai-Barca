FINANCING MODEL

Espai Barça will be the largest and most innovative sports complex in the heart of a European city. A unique project: up to €1.5 billion in investment, 18 hectares of land in a central location in a city like Barcelona, ​​and with 350,000 m2 of roofing.

Financing structure

Espai Barça is an essential project to keep FC Barcelona at the cutting-edge of the sports world. Therefore, an innovative financing structure has been designed that respects the three red lines approved in the referendum: Espai Barça finances itself through the income generated by the project itself, which means never mortgaging the Club's assets, never depending on the members for revenue, and never jeopardizing the sports management.

WITH ADDITIONAL REVENUES FROM
THE NEW PROJECT

Financing is based on the additional
income that the project will generate
thanks to new hospitality offerings,
sponsorships and title rights, the
Museum and its visits, ticketing
and catering, as well as the
management of new assets and
events generated by Espai Barça.

WITH GUARANTEES FROM ESPAI
BARÇA ITSELF

The Club has negotiated financing at an
estimated average net interest rate of
5.53%, with 20 international investors.

GRACE PERIOD DURING THE
CONSTRUCTION PHASE

During the construction period, and until
the completion of the renovation of the
Stadium in 2026, the principal is not
repaid. In addition, 100% of the
construction costs and interest during
the work, which began on June 1, 2023,
are guaranteed.

MOBILIZATION OF UP TO €1.5 BILLION

At the Assemblies held on October 17
and 23, 2020, the commissioners
approved by a wide majority the
authorization to carry out the necessary
actions to subscribe to an operation to
finance up to €1.5 billion. This figure was
also subsequently approved by the
members in the referendum of
December 19, 2021.
